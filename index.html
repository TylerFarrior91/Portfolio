    • About Me

    Hello there! I'm Tyler Farrior, an automotive technician with a strong background in automotive quality. With a keen understanding of automotive repairs and a knack for finding bad defects, I specialize in identifying root causes of quality problems. I had the incredible opportunity to contribute to Rivian's production startup, specifically on their R1-T truck, where I played a vital role in ensuring top-notch quality and precise body fitting work. While my automotive expertise has been a significant part of my journey, I've recently embarked on an exciting career transition into software development. Currently enrolled at Vanderbilt University's coding boot camp, I'm eagerly expanding my skills and knowledge in the ever-evolving world of technology. Beyond my professional pursuits, my most cherished accomplishment is being married to the most amazing wife. She is my rock, providing unwavering support and encouragement as I navigate through life's adventures. Moreover, I'm a proud father to two wonderful kids who constantly inspire me to strive for excellence and embrace new challenges. With a combination of automotive expertise, leadership skills, and my passion for coding, I'm excited to leverage my diverse background to drive innovation and deliver exceptional results. Join me on this journey as we push boundaries and make a positive impact together.
